Privacy policy

How authentic is this profile? Profile Prism's purpose is to score visible LinkedIn profile authenticity evidence on an explainable 0–100 index, locally and temporarily in browser memory.

No retention or transmission of profile information

The extension does not retain or transmit profile content, profile URLs, names, photographs, scores, cookies, authentication data, messages, or contact information. It has no backend, analytics, telemetry, advertising, remote configuration, or outbound runtime requests.

Information stored locally

Only extension preferences, the extension version, and the scoring ruleset version are stored in the browser's local extension storage. Profile-derived information is cleared when the page changes, closes, or the extension is disabled.

User-triggered complete scan

The initial estimate is automatic. Only after you press Click to verify authenticity, the extension may temporarily open and close LinkedIn's native “About this member” dialog and scroll the current profile to load more visible sections. It reads structured verification methods, account-history dates, and maintenance recency shown there, but never retains raw dialog text, organization names, URLs, or profile identifiers. You can cancel the scan at any time.

Purpose and limits

The score summarizes visible authenticity signals. It is not identity verification, a determination of fraud, or an assessment of messages, links, offers, or scam behavior. Do not use it as the sole basis for employment, recruiting, reporting, blocking, or trust decisions.
